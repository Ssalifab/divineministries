About Our Church

Divine Power Of Christ Ministries is a non-denominational church of Christ based on the foundation of holiness and Righteousness.

The ministry's vision is to reflect God's power through the teachings of Christ. Founded in 2008, we have been a beacon of hope and faith in our city for over 18 years.

Our mission is to lead people into a growing relationship with Jesus Christ through worship, discipleship, and service. We believe in the power of prayer, the importance of community, and the transformative work of the Holy Spirit.

Whether you're new to faith or have been walking with God for years, you'll find a welcoming community at Divine Ministries where you can grow spiritually and connect with others on the same journey.
